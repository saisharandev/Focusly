F-01

AI presence tracking ↗

Your laptop camera quietly counts seconds spent looking at your screen. Nothing leaves the device.

F-02

Synced pomodoro ↗

Everyone in the room is on the same 25/5. When the bell rings, the room rests together.

F-03

Live group rooms ↗

Public rooms by class code, private rooms by invite. Drop in for 20 minutes, or stay until 4am.

F-04

Leaderboards & streaks ↗

Compete with your dorm, your major, or your roommate. Your streak is yours. No streak-shield paywalls.

F-05

Analytics dashboard ↗

Heatmap of your focus by hour of day. Brutally honest about your 2pm slump. Use it.

F-06

Study music ↗

Lo-fi, brown noise, café murmur, or the actual sound of a library at 11pm. No ad breaks.

03 / privacy

Your camera never leaves your laptop.

Presence detection runs on-device
in WebAssembly. Nothing is stored.

On-device only

Frame data is processed locally with MediaPipe. No bytes hit our servers.

One number, not a face

The room sees a presence percentage. No video. No screenshots. Ever.

Camera-off mode

Skip the camera entirely. You still appear in the room and on the leaderboard. Focus score won't be calculated — that's fine.

Open detection model

Runs on Google's open-source MediaPipe models. The detection algorithm itself is fully public and auditable.
